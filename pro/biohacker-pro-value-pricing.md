# Biohacker Pro: Lifetime Unlock & Pricing Strategy

## 1. The Core Problem
Most health tracking apps (Apple Health, Oura, Whoop) lock your data behind monthly subscriptions and "neon" dashboards that look great on a screen but mean nothing in a clinical setting. When you take these apps to a doctor, they're often dismissed as consumer-grade noise.

## 2. The Solution: Biohacker Pro
Biohacker Pro is a one-time, lifetime unlock for `applehealthdata.com` that shifts the focus from "tracking" to "clinical auditing." It bridges the gap between your iPhone and your physician's office with a "Medical Journal" aesthetic and industry-standard data mapping.

## 3. Value Proposition: The "Clinical Edge"

### A. Physician-Ready PDF Reports
- **Clinical Elegance**: Reports designed with the Charter serif font and a clean, high-contrast medical layout. No fluff.
- **Standardized Codes**: Every observation is tagged with LOINC and ICD-10 codes, making it immediately readable for healthcare providers.
- **Audit-Ready**: Includes raw data logs alongside synthesized trends for full transparency.

### B. Advanced Biohacker Modules
- **HRV Correlation Matrix**: Analyze how your sleep quality, activity levels, and symptoms correlate with your Heart Rate Variability.
- **Anomalies Detection**: Automated flagging of values outside of established clinical ranges (e.g., blood pressure spikes or respiratory changes).
- **Long-Term Trend Analysis**: View 12+ month trends without the storage limits of the standard Apple Health interface.

### C. Absolute Data Ownership
- **No Subscriptions**: One payment of $199. No recurring charges.
- **Local-First Processing**: Your health data is processed in your browser. It never touches our servers.
- **Portable Formats**: Export your data in JSON, CSV, and Physician-Ready PDF at any time.
