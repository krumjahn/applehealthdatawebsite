Getting Started

This guide walks you through the complete setup: installing the iPhone app to export your Apple Health data, transferring it to your Mac, and running your first AI-powered analysis. The entire process takes under 5 minutes.

All data stays on your devices. Nothing is uploaded to any server at any point in this workflow.

Overview

The Health Data Export & AI Analyzer toolkit consists of two apps that work together:

  • iPhone app — Reads your Apple Health data and exports it in clean, analysis-ready formats (CSV, JSON, or XML).
  • Mac app — Imports the exported data, visualizes it with interactive charts, and runs local AI analysis to surface trends and insights.

By the end of this guide, you will have exported your health data from your iPhone, transferred it to your Mac, and generated your first AI-powered health summary.

Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have the following:

Requirement Details
iPhone Running iOS 16 or later
Mac Running macOS 13 (Ventura) or later
Apple Health data At least a few days of recorded data (steps, heart rate, sleep, etc.)
Same Wi-Fi network Required only if using Wi-Fi sync (AirDrop and cloud transfer work independently)

Step 1: Install the iPhone App

1

Download Health Data Export & AI Analyzer from the App Store on your iPhone.

Download from the App Store

When prompted, grant the app permission to read your Apple Health data. The app only reads data locally and never sends it anywhere.

Step 2: Export Your Apple Health Data

2

Open the Health Data Export app on your iPhone and follow these steps:

  1. Select the data categories you want to export (or choose "Select All" for a complete export).
  2. Choose your preferred date range (last 30 days, last year, or all time).
  3. Pick an output format: CSV for spreadsheets, JSON for development, or XML for maximum compatibility.
  4. Tap Export and wait for the file to be generated.

For a detailed walkthrough of all export options, see the Export Apple Health Data guide.

Step 3: Transfer to Your Mac

3

Once your export is complete, transfer the file to your Mac using any of these methods:

  • AirDrop (recommended) — Tap the Share button after export and select your Mac from the AirDrop targets. The file arrives in your Downloads folder within seconds.
  • Wi-Fi Sync — If both devices are on the same network, the iPhone app can send data directly to the Mac app. See the Wi-Fi Sync guide for setup instructions.
  • Cloud storage — Save the export to iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or Dropbox, then open it on your Mac.

Step 4: Import into the Mac App

4

Open the Health Data Export & AI Analyzer Mac app and import your data:

  1. Drag and drop the exported file directly onto the app window, or use File > Import from the menu bar.
  2. The app will parse your data and display a summary of the imported records, including category counts and date range.
  3. Once imported, your data appears in the dashboard with interactive charts for steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts, and more.

Step 5: Run Your First AI Analysis

5

With your data loaded in the Mac app, you are ready to generate your first AI-powered health summary:

  1. Navigate to the AI Analysis tab in the Mac app.
  2. Choose an AI provider: use the built-in local model, or connect to ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini with your own API key.
  3. Select a prompt template (such as "Weekly Health Summary" or "Sleep Quality Analysis") or write your own question.
  4. Click Analyze and wait for the results.

The AI processes your data locally on your Mac and returns a plain-language summary with trends, insights, and actionable suggestions.
