Health Data Export & AI Analyzer

Welcome to the documentation for Health Data Export & AI Analyzer — the privacy-first toolkit for exporting, syncing, converting, and analyzing your Apple Health data on iPhone and Mac.

All data processing happens locally on your devices. Your health data is never uploaded to any server.

What Can You Do?

Getting Started

Install the app, export your data, and run your first analysis in under 5 minutes.

iPhone-to-Mac Sync

Sync Apple Health data directly over your local network — no export files needed.

Convert Tool

Convert Apple Health XML to CSV, JSON, or Excel directly in your browser.

OpenClaw Integration

Talk to your health data with AI. Get daily briefs and suggestions from OpenClaw.

API Reference

Read-only localhost API for developers, scripts, and custom integrations.

AI Analysis

Use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or local LLMs to analyze your health data.
