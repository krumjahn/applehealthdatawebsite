How to Export Apple Health Sleep Data

Use this quick guide to export sleep records from Apple Health and turn them into clear trends you can actually use.

3-step workflow

  1. Export your Apple Health file on iPhone.
  2. Convert to CSV for filtering and charts.
  3. Run AI prompts to summarize sleep consistency and anomalies.

AI-ready prompts

  • Analyze my sleep duration trend by week and identify unusually short nights.
  • Find bedtime consistency patterns and suggest one improvement.
  • Create a weekly sleep scorecard with risk flags and one action for next week.
