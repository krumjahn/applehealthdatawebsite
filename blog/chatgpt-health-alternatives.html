Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChatGPT Health safe to use?

ChatGPT Health is relatively safe, but your data is transmitted to OpenAI's servers and subject to their policies and legal obligations. It's not HIPAA-compliant for consumer use.

Can I use multiple tools together?

Yes! Many power users combine ChatGPT/Claude for quick questions with local analysis tools for comprehensive privacy-focused deep dives. This hybrid approach offers both convenience and control.

What's the most private way to analyze health data?

Local-only analysis tools that never transmit your data to any server. All processing happens on your own Mac or iPhone, ensuring complete privacy.

How much do local analysis tools cost?

Typically $5-15 as a one-time purchase. Compare this to $240-$2,400/year for ChatGPT Plus/Pro or Claude Pro/Max subscriptions.

Do I need technical skills for local analysis?

Not necessarily. Basic spreadsheet analysis requires no coding. Python-based tools are more powerful but have a learning curve. Choose based on your comfort level.

Can I still use AI with local tools?

Yes! You can export your data, then upload specific anonymized subsets to ChatGPT/Claude when needed. Or use local AI models (Ollama, LM Studio) for complete privacy.
