App Mini-Reviews

Honest takes on the most notable alternatives

Health Auto Export

Power Users

The most powerful automation tool in this category. If you want to push health data to Home Assistant, a REST API, or MQTT broker automatically, nothing else comes close. Steep learning curve for non-technical users, and pricing isn't transparent on the website.

Health Export CSV

Spreadsheets

A solid workhorse for CSV exports with a useful remote web dashboard feature. Supports 60+ data types and integrates with Apple Shortcuts for automation. Missing Mac sync and AI-specific output formats.

AI Health Export

AI-focused

Purpose-built for AI analysis: 93 metrics exported as AI-ready CSV with built-in prompt guides for ChatGPT and Claude. The $14.99 lifetime deal is great value. No Mac sync, JSON-only crowd won't love it.

Lode: Health Export for AI

Free

Free and thoughtfully designed — exports 92 metrics as enriched JSON optimized specifically for AI context windows, even showing token count before export. JSON-only limits spreadsheet users, and requires iOS 26.

Health2AI - Export for ChatGPT

AI chatbots

Clever Markdown export format makes it easy to paste directly into any AI chat. Multi-AI support (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini). Basic feature set beyond exporting — no Mac sync or automation.

Heart Reports

Medical

Great for users who need medical-grade PDF reports to share with doctors. Six specialized report types covering heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar, sleep, activity, and oxygen. Very focused — not a general-purpose export tool.

Health App Data Export Tool

Reports

Free to try with a clean interface for generating PDF and CSV reports. Good for creating shareable health summaries. The premium bundle pricing ($63) feels steep for what it offers.

Health Lens - CSV Exporter

Open Source

Completely free and open source under the MIT license — rare in this category. Exports all quantity types to CSV compatible with Excel, Python, and R. No frills: no PDF, no AI features, no Mac sync.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export Apple Health data to CSV?

Download an app like Health Data Export & AI Analyzer, open it, select the metrics and date range you want, and tap Export. The app generates a CSV file you can open in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets.

Can I export Apple Health data to my Mac?

Yes — Health Data Export & AI Analyzer includes a Mac companion app that syncs your data via Wi-Fi or iCloud/Google Drive. It's the only export app with native Mac sync.

Which Apple Health export app works with ChatGPT?

Health Data Export & AI Analyzer, AI Health Export, Lode, and Health2AI all produce AI-friendly output. Our app supports CSV, JSON, and XML — giving you the most flexibility for different AI tools.

Is there a free Apple Health export app?

Yes. Health Lens is free and open source. Health Data Export is free with tips. For a fully-featured free experience, Health Data Export & AI Analyzer has a free tier you can try before purchasing.

Does any Apple Health export app support Oura Ring data?

Health Data Export & AI Analyzer is currently the only Apple Health export app that also supports Oura Ring and Samsung Galaxy Ring data, letting you consolidate all your health data in one export.
