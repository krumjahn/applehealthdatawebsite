What is Copilot Health for Apple Health users?

Microsoft Copilot Health is an AI health experience for asking health questions and exploring medical information. For Apple Health users, the important distinction is simple: Copilot can explain health concepts, but it does not automatically turn years of iPhone HealthKit data into private CSV, JSON, charts, or local trend summaries.

Best workflow: export first, ask Copilot second

  1. Export Apple Health data from iPhone as CSV or JSON.
  2. Run local analysis on Mac to summarize sleep, steps, workouts, heart rate, HRV, and other metrics.
  3. Copy only the relevant summary into Copilot Health if you want a plain-English explanation or research follow-up.
  4. Keep the raw Apple Health export local unless you explicitly choose to upload it.

FAQ

Can Copilot Health read Apple Health data directly?

Not as a complete private Apple Health export workflow. You still need to export, clean, and summarize your data before an AI assistant can reason over it well.

What should I send to Copilot?

Send a compact summary: date range, metrics, averages, trend changes, and the exact question you want answered. Avoid uploading the full export unless you are comfortable with cloud processing.
