Perplexity Health Analysis: Using AI to Decode Your Data

While ChatGPT and Claude are popular for analysis, Perplexity offers a unique advantage: the ability to cross-reference your personal Apple Health data with the latest medical research in real-time.

Perplexity's "Research" mode can help explain why certain trends in your heart rate or sleep might be occurring based on recent studies.

Step 1: Export as CSV or JSON

Perplexity handles structured data files exceptionally well. Use the Health Data Export app on your iPhone to generate a CSV or JSON file. CSV is generally preferred for trend analysis.

Step 2: Upload to Perplexity

Start a new thread in Perplexity and upload your exported file. We recommend using Pro mode (selecting Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o) for the most accurate data parsing.

The "Research-First" Prompt

Use this prompt pattern to combine your data with external medical knowledge:

"I have uploaded my Apple Health data covering the last 90 days. 1. Analyze the correlation between my sleep duration and my heart rate variability (HRV). 2. Search for recent medical research regarding the impact of late-night meals on these specific metrics. 3. Based on my data and your search results, suggest 3 actionable changes to improve my recovery."

Why use Perplexity over ChatGPT?

The main benefit is citations. When Perplexity tells you that your resting heart rate is slightly above average for your age group, it can cite the specific health guidelines it's using for that comparison.
