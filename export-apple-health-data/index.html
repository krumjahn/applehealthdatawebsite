Method 1: The Official Apple Health Export (The Hard Way)

Apple provides a built-in function to export your data. It's comprehensive, but the output is not user-friendly.

  1. Open the Health App on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top-right corner.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Export All Health Data.
  4. Confirm by tapping Export and wait for the process to complete.
  5. When the Share Sheet appears, save the health-records.zip file.
What you get: Inside the ZIP file is export.xml, a massive and complex file. This file is difficult to use without technical expertise and usually requires a secondary process to convert the Apple Health XML to a usable format like CSV.
